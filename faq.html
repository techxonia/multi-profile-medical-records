General

It is an offline-based app that lets you manage medical records for your whole family. You can store doctor visits, medications, diseases, medical tests, surgeries, vaccines, medical insurance, doctor details, prescriptions, and attach documents — all in one secure place on your device.
Yes. The app is available on the Google Play Store for Android and the Apple App Store for iPhone and iPad.
No. The app is fully offline. All your data is stored locally on your device. An internet connection is only used for ads and premium purchase verification.
Yes. The app supports multiple languages including English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Dutch, Polish, Portuguese, and Russian. You can switch the language from Settings.

Profiles & Data

Yes. You can create unlimited profiles for yourself and every family member, each with their own medical records.
Free users can add a limited number of records per category. Premium users can add unlimited medications, doctor visits, tests, surgeries, and any other records per profile with no restrictions.
Yes. You can capture a photo with your camera or pick one from your gallery and attach it to any record (prescriptions, test reports, etc.).
When adding a profile, tap the contact icon in the Full Name field. The app will ask for contact permission, then let you pick a contact. The name, photo, and phone number will be filled automatically.

Security & Privacy

Absolutely. All data is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is transmitted to any server. We have no access to your information.
Go to Settings > App Lock. You can set a 4-digit PIN and optionally enable biometric (fingerprint / Face ID) authentication. The app will require authentication every time you open it.
If you forget your PIN and biometric is not set up, you will need to clear the app data (which erases all records) or restore from a backup if you have one.

Backup & Restore

You can create a local backup file containing all your data. This file is saved to your device storage. You can then copy it to a computer or cloud drive for safekeeping.
Transfer your backup file to the new phone, open the app, go to Settings > Backup & Restore, and select the file. Restoring replaces all current data in the app and resets the PIN to what it was at the time of backup.
Cross-platform transfer is not currently supported. Backup files are specific to the platform they were created on.

Subscription & Premium

Premium removes all ads, unlocks unlimited backup files, and gives you access to all current and future premium features.
You can choose a yearly subscription ($9.99/year) or a one-time Lifetime purchase ($29.99). Both give you the same premium features.
On Android, manage your subscription in the Google Play Store. On iOS, go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.
Subscription and purchase amounts are strictly non-refundable under any condition. Please review the premium features before purchasing.
Open the app and go to the Premium/Upgrade screen. Tap “Restore Purchase.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Google account (Android) or Apple ID (iOS) that you used for the original purchase. Your premium access will be restored automatically.

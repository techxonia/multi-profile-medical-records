FAQ

Q. How to edit and delete data?

A. You have to slide the item from left to right and vice versa, you will see the corresponding edit & delete button. Click respective button to edit or delete the data. 

Q. Can i add unlimited data on Digital Locker app?

A. Yes, you can add multiple data to the Digital Locker app.

Q. How to enable Clipboard Automatic Paste feature in Digital Locker App.?

A. On the Settings tab, you will see the Clipboard section. Clicking on the "Automatic Paste" button will redirect you to the Accessibility screen. Search the Digital Locker application and enable the service for automatic pasting feature.

How Data Removal Works

Multi-Profile Medical Records is a fully offline application. We want to be completely clear about what that means for your data:

  • The app does not create or maintain online accounts — there is no registration, login, or cloud account to delete.
  • All of your medical records and profiles are stored exclusively on your device.
  • Because nothing is kept on our servers, deleting your data is done directly within the app — instantly and entirely under your control.

Steps to Delete Your Data

Follow these steps inside the app to permanently remove everything stored on your device:

1

Open the App

Launch Multi-Profile Medical Records on your device.

2

Go to More

Navigate to the More section.

3

Scroll Down

Scroll to the bottom of the screen.

4

Tap Delete

Tap “Delete All Data & Reset App” (shown as Log Out in some versions).

5

Confirm

Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Result: All profiles, medical records, reminders, and locally stored information are permanently removed from your device.

Alternative Method

Prefer to remove everything at once? You can also simply uninstall the application:

  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • No further action is required — because no server-side account exists, there is nothing left to delete elsewhere.

Deletion is permanent. If you want to keep a copy of your records, export or back them up before deleting or uninstalling the app.

Our Privacy Commitment

Your privacy is at the core of how this app is built:

  • Your medical records remain on your device.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not maintain cloud copies of your medical records.
  • We do not share your health information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I delete my account?

Multi-Profile Medical Records does not create online accounts. All information is stored locally on your device.

How do I permanently remove my data?

Use the Delete All Data option within the app, or uninstall the application.

Do you keep copies of my medical records?

No. Medical records remain on your device unless you manually export or back them up.

How long does deletion take?

Deletion occurs immediately on your device.

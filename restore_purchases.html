Restore Purchases

Multi-Profile Medical Records App — restore your Premium access on a new device, after reinstalling, or when your purchase isn’t showing.

Overview

If you previously bought Premium — either the auto-renewable yearly subscription or the one-time Lifetime purchase — you can restore it for free at any time. Use Restore Purchases when:

  • You installed the app on a new phone or tablet.
  • You deleted and reinstalled the app.
  • Your Premium features are not showing even though you already paid.

Restoring is free and never charges you again. You must be signed in with the same store account (Apple ID or Google account) you used for the original purchase.

iOS (Apple App Store)

Sign in to your device with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase, then open the app.

Restore a Lifetime purchase

  1. Open the Multi-Profile Medical Records app.
  2. Go to Settings (or the Premium / Upgrade screen).
  3. Tap Restore Purchases.
  4. Wait for the confirmation — your Lifetime Premium unlocks automatically.

Restore a Subscription

  1. Open the app and tap Restore Purchases on the Premium / Upgrade screen.
  2. Your active yearly subscription is detected and Premium is re-enabled.
  3. To review or manage the subscription, open Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions.

An expired or canceled subscription cannot be restored — you would need to subscribe again. Only an active subscription is restored.

Android (Google Play)

Sign in to your device with the same Google account used for the original purchase, then open the app.

Restore a Lifetime purchase

  1. Open the Multi-Profile Medical Records app.
  2. Go to Settings (or the Premium / Upgrade screen).
  3. Tap Restore Purchases.
  4. Your one-time Lifetime Premium is detected and unlocked.

Restore a Subscription

  1. Open the app and tap Restore Purchases on the Premium / Upgrade screen.
  2. Your active yearly subscription is detected and Premium is re-enabled.
  3. To review or manage the subscription, open the Google Play Store → profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.

An expired or canceled subscription cannot be restored — you would need to subscribe again. Only an active subscription is restored.

Troubleshooting

If your purchase still doesn’t restore, check the following:

  • Correct account: confirm you’re signed in with the exact Apple ID / Google account used to buy Premium.
  • Internet connection: restoring requires a working connection to verify the purchase with the store.
  • Update the app: install the latest version from the App Store or Google Play, then try again.
  • Restart: fully close and reopen the app (or restart your device) and tap Restore Purchases again.
  • Subscription status: verify the subscription is still active in your store account — expired/canceled plans cannot be restored.
  • Still stuck? contact us with your store order/receipt ID and we’ll help.

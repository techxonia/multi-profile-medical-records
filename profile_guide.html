Profile Setup Guide

Q. How to register an app after installation?

Follow the step below:

  1. 1. Open Multi-Profile Medical Records Application from home screen.
  2. 2. You will see the "Tour" page. Press the Next button to go to the next page and get to the last page.
  3. 3. Press the "Let’s Go" button to redirect to the "Self Profile" registration page. You can press the "Skip" button to redirect to the "Self Profile" registration page.
  4. 4. You can enter your personal information in each field and press the forward arrow button to complete the “Self” profile registration page

Q. How to add a profile image?

A. At the top of the application, you will see an avatar. Press the plus icon closest to that avatar. If you press the plus icon, you will be prompted to permission dialog to grant file storage and camera. After accessing the permission, you will see a dialog. Choose a method to take or select a photo.

Q. How to fetch contact name ,photo and number from contact page?

A. You will see an add profile icon in the full name box field on the right side. Press the add profile icon and access the contact permission. You will be redirected to the contact list page. Click on one of the contacts. You will be back to the registration page and your full name, photo and contact number will be filled automatically. (Photo, Address, Name, Number will be autofilled if they are available on contact list).

Q. How to fetch a mobile number from a contact page?

A. You will see a contact icon in the Mobile Number box field on the right side. Press the contact icon and access the contact permission. You will be redirected to the contact list page. Click on one of the contacts. You will be back to the registration page and your contact number will be filled automatically.

Q. How to change mobile number country code?

A. Press on “Code” box field. Just left of Mobile Number. You will redirect to the next page. Search your country code and click. Your country code will be auto filled.

Q. How to set Date of Birth?

A. You can set your date of birth in two ways. One by manual typing and the next date picker. You must follow the date formatting pattern if you write manually.
i.e DD/MM/YYYY
D-> Day , M-> Month , Y-> Year

Q. How to set Gender?

A. Press on the “Gender” box field. Dialog will appear, click gender to set.

Q. How to set blood groups?

A. Press on the “Blood Group” box field. Dialog will appear, click the blood group to set.

Q. What formats are available for the weight unit?

A. “KG” & “LBS” .Press “Type” box field to change weight unit
  1. If you choose KG. And you type “55” in weight. Your format will be 55KG
  2. If you choose LBS. And you type “55.2” in weight. Your format will be 55.2LBS

Q. What formats are available for the height unit?

A. “FT” & “CM” .Press “Type” box field to change height unit.
  1. If you choose FT. And you type “5” in height. Your format will be 5 ft 0 in
  2. If you choose FT. And you type “5.2” in height. Your format will be 5 ft 2 in
  3. If you choose CM. And you type “135.2” in height. Your format will be 135.2cm
  4. If you choose CM. And you type “135” in height. Your format will be 135cm

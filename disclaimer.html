About the App

Multi-Profile Medical Records is an offline-based app to keep the details of your important cards, insurance, and clipboard notes in one place securely. You can store your ID card info such as Passport, Citizenship, Driving License, or Debit and Credit cards, vehicle and life insurance data, and manage your clipboard data.

Your data remains on your device locally. No single piece of information or data is ever sent to a server.
